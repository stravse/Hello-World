Dr. Kal'tsit

"She comes from the past. She belongs to the present"

current time line of Dr. Kal'tsit's life:

  • over 22 years ago - Warned Prof.Thorne about the dangers of his research but the professor ignores her
  • 22 years ago - Helped a boy named Elliot. The caravan that he was in was ambushed and Prof.Thorne died. Elliot decides to destroy the professor's research as it could end many lives at the wrong hands
  • 22 - 20 yrs ago - Became the lead researcher on the sarchopagus device on Chernobog. While leading the project became the mentor of well known scientist. The research made Chernobog to flourish.
  • 20 years ago - Discovered that the device could generate more energy than current output. Argues whether continuing with the research would be ethical. The 4th army of Ursus infiltrated the facility and killed most of the researchers
  • 20 - 17 years ago - Was able to escape the facility and stayed on a rural village on the Tundra. Provided medical services to the poor people within the Region
  • 17 years ago - An old student found her. Talked about getting revenge for their fellow researchers on Chernobog
  • 17 - 15 years ago - Assassinated one of the leaders that gave the command to the 4th army of Ursus
  • 15 - 13 years ago - Became the mentor of an influential man known as Mr.Thompson on Victoria
  • 13 years ago - Received a letter from Theresa the King of Kazdel. The king wanted her to be her advisor.
  • 13 - 3 years ago - She came to Kazdel to serve the king and lead Babel a company established by the king to better the life of Kazdel. She met the heir of the king and her right hand man named Dokutah along her service.
  • 3 years ago - Theresa was murdered. Dokutah was heavily injured by the incident. She took his body and brought him to the Sarcophagus on Chernobog to be placed in Cryostasis to heal his wound. She left Chernobog to become the mentor of Theresa's heir and to lead what is left of Babel
  • 3 years ago - CURRENT Took what ever is left from Babel and established a pharmaceutical company named Rhodes Island. Moved from nation to nation bringing in infected people and to find the cure for the infection.
  • CURRENT A joint operation was established on Chernobog by the heir of Theresa to wake Dokutah from cryostasis
